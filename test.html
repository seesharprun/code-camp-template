Presenter: Sidney Andrews [SeeSharpRun.NET]

5 Years of MongoDB at Snagajob: A Giant Idea to Scaling The Worker Universe

5 Years ago, the Snagajob website ran on a single server (monolithic) relational database platform. Though the site ran stable and with excellent performance the challenge was to keep it that way while the growth of the site was actually accelerating. Scaling the Snagajob site essentially came down to scaling the OLTP data tier. At that point in time, the only option to scale the data tier was to scale up, i.e. deploy bigger and better infrastructure. Our concern was that the site was growing faster than scaling up would be able to support, and in hindsight, we were right.
